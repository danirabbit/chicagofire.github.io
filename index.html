Locations:

Chicago Fire was born of my desire to share the Unique flavors and textures of Chicago Pizza.

I began researching and making pizza after moving out west from Chicago. When I opened the first Chicago Fire, I created recipes based on how great they taste, not how much time they take to prepare.

Our menu is strongly influenced by Chicago’s history and culture. Many of Chicago’s tastiest foods were created by Greek and Italian immigrants. We offer numerous “Fiery” foods such as Extra Hot Wings, The Great Chicago Fire Pizza, Spicy Italian Sausage, and of course our signature Hot Italian Beef.

Most of our sandwiches and pizzas have over a half pound of meat, staying true to portions one would expect in The Windy City, once known as the pork capital of the world. Premium Wisconsin Mozzarella, separate thin and deep dish dough recipes prepared daily, and a third generation sausage purveyor from Chicago—the same sausage maker that supplied the pizzerias that made Chicago pizza famous—are just a few of the examples of my commitment to quality and authenticity.
